The Four Gospels and Acts in Inuinnaqtun

This is a digital copy of Godim Ukauhiit Gospelit Hitamanguyun Apostlit Havaangillu, or The Four Gospels and Acts in Copper Eskimo, published by the Canadian Bible Society in 1972. The text has been preserved as accurately as possible; only some punctuation and cross-references were changed during digitization.

If you are interested in obtaining a printed copy of this Bible, please contact the Canadian Bible Society at http://www.biblescanada.com.
